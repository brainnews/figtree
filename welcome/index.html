Thanks for installing Treekit

Let's get started by adding your first Figma project

1

Copy your Figma project link

Go to your Figma projects and copy the link to any project you want to add to Treekit.

Open your Figma projects open_in_new
2

Open Treekit

Open Treekit by clicking the extension icon in the top right corner of your browser.

3

Add your project to Treekit

Paste the Figma project link into the "Add Figma project by URL" input and press enter or click the add button. Treekit will automatically add the project to your list of projects.

4

That's it!

Now you can start using Treekit to link copy links to your Figma projects. You can also keep pin projects, pages, frames, or groups to the top of the list to keep them at the top of your list.
