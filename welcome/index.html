Welcome to Treekit

Quick access to your Figma projects from anywhere on the web. Let's get you set up!

1

Find a Figma project

Open your Figma projects page and locate any project you'd like to access quickly. Right-click on the project and select "Copy link" from the menu.

Open your Figma projects open_in_new
2

Open Treekit

Click the Treekit extension icon in your browser's toolbar (top-right corner). If you don't see it, click the puzzle piece icon to find it in your extensions.

3

Add your project

Paste your copied project link into the input field at the top of Treekit and press Enter. Your project will appear in the list below.

4

Start exploring!

Now you can browse your project's pages and frames, copy direct links to any element, and pin your favorites to the top. Treekit works on any website, giving you instant access to your Figma projects wherever you are.
